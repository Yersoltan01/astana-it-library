Description: today, the World Public Library Collection has more than 3,000,000+ PDF books in 100+ languages, containing 125 of the best e-books and eDocument collections published on the Internet. Description: free search engine for full texts of scientific publications of all formats and disciplines. The project has been running since November 2004, initially in beta status. Website: doabooks.org

Description: The main goal of DOAB is to increase the clarity of open access books. Academic Publishers are invited to submit the metadata of their open books to DOAB Access. Metadata will be harvested in order to maximize dissemination, visibility and impact. Drives can integrate records into their commercial services and libraries can integrate a catalog into their online catalogs, helping scholars and students discover books.
